# HotelOS Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

HotelOS is a premium hospitality technology platform focused on hotel management, hotel booking, revenue optimization, and guest experiences.

Brand Personality:

* Premium
* Luxury
* Trustworthy
* Modern
* Enterprise
* Sophisticated
* Minimalist

---

# Primary Brand Colors

## Primary Navy

HEX: #1B3B5D

Purpose:

* Primary brand color
* Logo
* Headings
* Navbar
* Footer
* Sidebar
* Primary buttons
* Icons

Represents:
Trust, professionalism, stability, technology

---

## Luxury Gold

HEX: #C5A059

Purpose:

* Accent color
* Premium highlights
* CTA emphasis
* Ratings
* Badges
* Active states

Represents:
Luxury, exclusivity, premium hospitality

Rule:
Use sparingly.
Gold should never dominate the interface.

# Brand Color Ratio

For Branding Materials:

60% Navy (#1B3B5D)

30% Off White (#FAFAFA)

10% Gold (#C5A059)

---

# Website Color Ratio

70% White / Off White

20% Navy

10% Gold

Reason:
Luxury hotel websites should feel clean, spacious, and premium.
Too much dark color makes the experience feel heavy.

# Design Principles

* Use white space generously
* Gold is an accent, not a primary color
* Prioritize readability over decoration
* Maintain a luxury hospitality feel
* Avoid excessive gradients
* Avoid neon colors
* Prefer subtle animations
* Keep interfaces clean and premium
